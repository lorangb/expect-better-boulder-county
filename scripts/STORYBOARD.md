# Campaign Video Storyboard
## "We Built This Place. Let's Protect It."
### 30 seconds · 5 clips · Colorado mountain aesthetic

---

### 🎬 CLIP 1 — Dawn Over the Foothills (6 sec)
**Visual:** Aerial drone shot of Colorado foothills at golden hour. Mist over pine forests and red rock. Warm light.
**Narration:** "Decades ago, people came here because they believed a different way was possible."
**Vibe:** Hopeful, expansive, nostalgic

### 🏭 CLIP 2 — The Dark Smokestack (6 sec)
**Visual:** Slow push-in on the CEMEX plant smokestack against blue sky. Mountain backdrop visible. White smoke rising.
**Narration:** "But while we built this community, a coal-fired plant ran in our backyard. 1969 standards. 55 years of emissions."
**Vibe:** Sobering, contrast, truth

### 🚛 CLIP 3 — Trucks on Highway 66 (6 sec)
**Visual:** Dump trucks rumbling through Lyons one after another. Dust. Small town street overwhelmed.
**Narration:** "Truck traffic jumped 17x. Our kids breathe this air. Our neighbors sit in this dust."
**Vibe:** Urgent, real, visceral

### 📋 CLIP 4 — The People / The Evidence (6 sec)
**Visual:** Montage — hands flipping through documents, a person at a laptop studying data, a community meeting with hands raised.
**Narration:** "But we did the work. The scientists measured. The watchdogs tracked. The attorneys built the case. The evidence is public."
**Vibe:** Earnest, capable, documented

### ⚖️ CLIP 5 — Call to Action (6 sec)
**Visual:** Boulder County government building. Clouds parting. Golden sunlight breaking through. Hopeful.
**Narration:** "The Board of Adjustment hearing is our moment. We need you to show up one more time. Because if not us, who? And if not now, when?"
**Text overlay:** "Expect Better for Boulder County"
**Vibe:** Inspiring, urgent, mobilizing
